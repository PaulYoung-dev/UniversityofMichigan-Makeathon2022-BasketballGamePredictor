What Being a Game Designer Aboard WolverineSoft Studio Involves

As a Game Designer, these are some principles to remember.

Confluence

To work as a team we utilize Confluence, an effective document-organizing software that enables easy group collaboration.

Limits

As an aspiring game-creator I refuse to lock my potential through internal doubts. Impact is a choice that ignores self-defeating voices of uncertainty.

Idea Optimization

In order to come up with the best ideas we gain advice from people in other departments for feedback to continuously revise iterations.

Discord

To communicate rapidly and effectively with one another we utilize Discord, having our ideas and suggestions organized in various servers.

File Organization

By using Git Pages, we are able to work as a team and keep track of project revision history.

Non-Profit Organization Project

WolverineSoft Studio is a non-profit organization, so the gems we create are to encourage others that you can make your games a reality.
